Link to the database

The database used to train the autoencoder is Flickr-Faces-HQ (FFHQ), a high-quality image dataset of human faces.
It was originally created as a benchmark for generative adversarial networks (GAN):
            A Style-Based Generator Architecture for Generative Adversarial Networks,
            Tero Karras (NVIDIA), Samuli Laine (NVIDIA), Timo Aila (NVIDIA)
            http://stylegan.xyz/paper

It is available at the following link:
            https://github.com/NVlabs/ffhq-dataset.git
